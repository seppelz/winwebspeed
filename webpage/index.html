Powerful Features

Everything you need to monitor your network and system performance

Real-Time Monitoring

Get instant updates on your network speeds every second. See your download and upload rates in real-time with precise measurements.

CPU & RAM Monitoring

Monitor your CPU usage percentage, temperature, and RAM usage alongside network stats. See which processes are consuming the most CPU and memory resources. Smart filtering excludes system processes like "System Idle Process" and "Memory Compress" so you see actual applications. Keep an eye on system performance at a glance.

Smart Taskbar Overlay

Always-visible stats right in your taskbar. Shows network speeds, CPU usage, RAM usage, and top processes. Automatically adjusts size based on enabled features - compact mode when only network stats are shown, full width when CPU and RAM are enabled. Progress bars intelligently match text width to save space. No need to open windows or check system tray.

Detailed Statistics

Open the detailed window to see total data transferred, connection type, monitoring duration, CPU/RAM usage with top processes, and customizable display options. More detailed than the compact taskbar overlay.

Fully Customizable

Choose your preferred units (kbps, Mbit/s, MB/s), set max speed for progress bars, and toggle visibility of CPU stats, RAM stats, and progress indicators. Control what appears in both the stats window and taskbar overlay.

Lightweight & Fast

Minimal resource usage. WebSpeed runs efficiently in the background without impacting your system performance.

Why Always Monitor Your Network?

Stay informed and secure with real-time network visibility

Security & Malware Detection

Detect suspicious activity instantly. If you notice your upload speed is unexpectedly high while you're not doing anything, it could be a sign of malware, spyware, or unauthorized data transmission. WebSpeed gives you the visibility to catch these threats early before they cause serious damage.

Malicious software often sends data in the background without your knowledge. With WebSpeed always visible in your taskbar, you'll immediately notice unusual network activity and can take action to protect your privacy.

Know If Content Is Actually Loading

Ever wondered if your video stream is buffering or if a download is stuck? WebSpeed shows you exactly what's happening. See real-time download speeds to know if your content is loading properly or if there's a connection issue that needs attention.

No more guessing whether your browser is working or if you need to refresh. The numbers don't lie â€“ you'll see immediately if data is flowing or if something needs troubleshooting.

See Your Actual Browsing Speed

Understand your real internet performance as you surf. See how fast pages load, how quickly files download, and whether you're getting the speeds you're paying for from your ISP.

Track your connection quality throughout the day. Identify peak usage times, notice when speeds drop, and verify if your internet service is performing as advertised.

Monitor Data Usage

Keep track of how much data you're transferring. WebSpeed shows total bytes downloaded and uploaded since monitoring started, helping you stay within data caps and understand your usage patterns.

Troubleshoot Connection Issues

Quickly identify network problems. If speeds suddenly drop to zero or become inconsistent, you'll know immediately that there's an issue with your connection, router, or service provider.

Optimize Your Network

Make informed decisions about your network usage. See which activities consume the most bandwidth, identify background processes that might be slowing you down, and optimize your internet experience.

About WebSpeed

WebSpeed was created to provide a simple, elegant solution for monitoring network and system performance on Windows. Unlike heavy monitoring tools, WebSpeed focuses on what matters: giving you real-time visibility into your network speeds, CPU usage, RAM usage, and top processes without getting in your way. With smart filtering that excludes system processes like "System Idle Process" and "Memory Compress", you see the actual applications consuming your resources.

Open Source

Built with transparency. View the code, contribute, or customize to your needs.

Privacy First

All monitoring happens locally. No data collection, no telemetry, no internet required.
